Pad Thai

Ref Picture

The authentic Thai dish by you

InGredients

  • Rice Noodle
  • Egg
  • Tamarine Juice
  • Oil
  • Shrimp
  • Peanut
  • Beansprout

    • Directions

    1. Spread oil in a pan 1/8 cup
    2. Pan fry shrimp to medium rare and take it out
    3. Cook noodle to soft by adding some water
    4. Add Tamarine Juice
    5. Add egg and shrimp
    6. Serve in a dish top with Peanut and beansprout on the side
